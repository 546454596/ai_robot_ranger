使用过程：
1.打开仿真环境
roslaunch ranger_mini_gazebo ai_robot_ranger_gazebo.launch
2.载入地图，打开AMCL定位
roslaunch ai_robot_nav_demo brainnav.launch path:=true, 但要注释掉<!--include file="$(find nav_demo)/launch/path.launch" /-->

3.打开基于拓扑地图的全局导航
roslaunch ai_robot_navigation globalnavsim.launch
4.打开局部避障
roslaunch ai_robot_navigation obsavoidsim.launch
